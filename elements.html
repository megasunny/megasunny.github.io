Document Embedding

We employ SPECTER model to represent titles and abstracts into the low dimensional space, to prepare for the analysis of paper topics and follow-up studies.

The left graph above shows the paper vectors in 7 clusters through UMAP dimensional reduction. The middle and the right graph are word cloud graphs of two of the 7 clusters.

Scientific Mobility

We apply representation techniques to represent the trajectories of scientists into the low dimensional space as well, to measure the similarities of affiliations and trajectories respectively.

Statistical analysis
