Statistical Graph Models

One of the projects in the course focus on the community detection in networks based on the probability model, which is the same as the mixture unigram model in natural language processing.

The left picture above illustrates the probability graph architecture of the mixture of unigram model. the right picture shows the adjacent matrix of the simulated network with 2 communities.

Categorical Data Analysis

High Dimensional Statistics

My work in the final project is concerned with Conic Programming and CCD algorithm to solve the square-root LASSO problem. simulated experiments are also carried out to compared the performances of square-root LASSO and LASSO.

In the left two figures, true effects are either 1 or -1, and both probabilities are equal to 0.5. In the middle two figures, true effects are generated from uniform distribution. In the right two figures, true effects are generated Laplace distribution. All the distributions have the same mean 0 and the same variance 3.
