Master Thesis

Document Embedding

We train language models that can accurately identify information about papers' topics. The results show that the balanced accuracy based on SPECTER and SVM is the highest, reaching 0.5729, with a balanced log loss of 1.3514.

Scientific Mobility

We apply embedding techniques as well to represent institutions in the low dimensional space based on the scientists' trajectories, to reveal the latent structure of institutions in mobility pattern. The graph below is the affiliation map of the top 10 countries.

Statistical analysis

Our empirical analysis suggests that moving distance, the publications, and citations of researchers have positive effects on the shift of research interests.

Bachelor Thesis

My bachelor thesis mainly focuses on short text clustering. The dataset contains titles and categories from Arxiv database. Both the LDA topic model and Word2vec-base models were implemented in our project. The reasons behind the distinction were also interpreted in my thesis.
