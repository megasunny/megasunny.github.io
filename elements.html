High Dimensional Statistics

My bachelor thesis mainly focuses on short text clustering. The dataset contains titles and categories from Arxiv database. Both the traditional topic model LDA and Word2vec-base models are implemented in our project. The reasons behind the distinction are also interpreted in my thesis.

The left and the middle right graph are word cloud graphs of two of the 2 categories. The right graph shows the performances of difference methods.
